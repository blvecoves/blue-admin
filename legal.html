BlueDev Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2nd, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how BlueDev collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you interact with the BlueDev Roblox group, our Roblox games ("Games"), our Discord server ("Server"), and specifically when you use the Blue Admin bot and in-game administration system ("Blue Admin" or "Service"). By engaging with BlueDev, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Services, Blue Admin collects the following types of information:

  • From Discord:
    • Discord User ID: To uniquely identify you and link to your Roblox account.
    • Discord Username & Server Nickname: For display within the Server and for administrative identification.
    • Server-specific Permissions & Roles: To manage access to channels and features.
    • Interaction Data (via Blue Admin bot): Logs of commands used (e.g., support commands, general commands), messages sent to the bot, and bot-related events for functionality, support, and moderation purposes.
    • Webhook Data: Information sent to Discord via webhooks for logging game events or administrative actions.
  • From Roblox (via public Open Cloud API and direct user input):
    • Roblox User ID: Provided by you during verification to uniquely link your Discord and Roblox accounts.
    • Roblox Username & Display Name: To set your nickname in the Server and for administrative identification in both Discord and Games.
    • Roblox Group Memberships & Ranks: To synchronize your Discord roles with your Roblox group status.
    • In-Game Interaction Data (via Blue Admin system): Logs of in-game administrative commands executed (e.g., bans, kicks, warnings), chat logs related to admin actions, and other gameplay data necessary for moderation and game management.
  • Combined Data: Your Discord and Roblox IDs are linked and stored together to facilitate cross-platform identity management and service delivery.

We do not collect or store your Roblox password or any other sensitive account login credentials. All Roblox account information shared is listed on the Blue Admin OAuth2 page and not released until further confirmation.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your collected information is used exclusively for the operation, maintenance, improvement, and security of BlueDev and its Services:

  • Verification & Linking: To confirm your ownership of a Roblox account and link it to your Discord profile.
  • Role & Nickname Management: To automatically assign/update your roles and nicknames in the Discord Server based on your Roblox group status.
  • Service Delivery: To enable features like support tickets, general commands, and webhook functionality on Discord.
  • Administration & Moderation: To facilitate and log administrative actions by staff within Discord and BlueDev Games/Group, including enforcing rules, issuing warnings, kicks, or bans. This helps maintain a safe and fair environment.
  • System Maintenance & Improvement: To monitor the performance, identify and fix issues, and develop new features for Blue Admin.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraudulent activities, abuse, or security breaches.
