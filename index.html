1. Information We Collect

To provide our Service, we collect and store certain information from both Discord and Roblox.

  • Information from Discord:
    • Your Discord User ID: To uniquely identify you and link you to your verified Roblox account.
    • Your Discord Username & Server Nickname: To identify you for use with other commands subject but not limited to administrative commands.
  • Information from Roblox (via their public Open Cloud API):
    • Your Roblox User ID: Provided by you during the verification process.
    • Your Roblox Username & Display Name: To set your nickname in our Server.
    • Your Roblox Group Memberships & Ranks: To sync your Roblox group roles with your roles in our Server.

We do not collect or store your Roblox password or any other sensitive account credentials. The verification process does not require you to provide a password. The information you share with us is visible to you and not released until your confirmation.

The information we collect is used exclusively to provide the Service's core functionality within our Server:

3. Data Storage and Security

4. Data Sharing
